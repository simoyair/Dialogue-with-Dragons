Frequently Asked Questions

No experience needed! We teach you everything step by step. Many of our students have never played D&D before. We focus on using D&D as a fun way to practice English, not on becoming D&D experts.
Our program is designed for B1-B2 level students. You should be able to have basic conversations in English but want to improve your confidence and fluency. We'll assess your level during the consultation.
We use Discord for voice chat and screen sharing. You'll need a computer or tablet with internet connection. We'll help you set everything up before your first session.
We understand life happens! You can reschedule with 24 hours notice. We also record key learning points so you won't miss important content.
Pricing varies by chapter. Early adopters get special founding member rates. We'll discuss exact pricing during your free consultation based on which chapters you're interested in.
