Why Learning English Through RPGs Really Works

Speak Without Fear

When you speak as your character, there's less pressure. You're focused on the story, not on being perfect. Research shows that learning through games reduces language anxiety. You're not worried about making mistakes - you're just playing your character and having fun!

Practice Like Real Life

RPGs create real communication challenges. You must listen, think, and answer in English - just like real conversations. Studies show students improve speaking ability when they practice English in real, unpredictable situations where they need to solve problems together.

Make Real Friends

Small groups create strong friendships. You're not just classmates - you're adventurers working together. Research shows that students in game-based learning create lasting connections and keep speaking English together. You gain both skills and community.

Meet Your Game Master and Teacher

I've been teaching for over 10 years in many different ways - from helping students with exams to training adults how to use new computer programs. For the past 5+ years, I've focused on teaching English conversation and helping students build real confidence speaking.

I discovered RPGs again in 2023 and immediately saw how perfect they are for language learning. When I run games, most of my players aren't native English speakers - and I watched them grow more confident week by week. I created Dialogue with Dragons to combine my two passions: teaching English and creating stories together.

I believe learning should feel exciting, not stressful. My goal is to create a safe space where you can experiment, make mistakes, and grow - not just in English, but as a person. When you speak as your character, you discover confidence you didn't know you had.

Whether you're saving dragons or fighting princesses, you'll use English naturally because you're focused on the adventure, not the grammar.
